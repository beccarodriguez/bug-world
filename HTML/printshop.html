Bella's Dream Map, 2017

Lithographs with chine colle, and video projection from Bobby Moulder's Motion Energy Analysis (MEA)
of Becca and their nibling, Bella (at age 3) drawing together.
Bella and Becca shared their recurring dreams with each other, then drew them out on a large piece of paper.
This was video documented, semi-transcribed, and then given to Bobby to process via MEA to show the moments Bella and Becca were most physically in sync.
Becca used MEA data, portraits, and quotes from their dream sequences to produce each lithograph, meditating
on things we can measure, and things we cannot, though we try.
